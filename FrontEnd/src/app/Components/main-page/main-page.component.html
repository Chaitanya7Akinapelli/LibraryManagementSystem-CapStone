Discover, borrow, and immerse yourself in the world of knowledge. Manage your books efficiently and never miss a due date.

Admin Roles: The admin is the backbone of our library management system, performing essential tasks:

  • Add books to the library collection.
  • Delete books that are no longer available.
  • Mark the return of books borrowed by users.
  • Search for books by title, author, or ISBN.
  • Generate reports on borrowing activities and overdue penalties.
